How To Grow Your Business SO Fast It Feels Illegal

11.2K

Six growth levers — pricing beats marketing.

If you're running a side hustle, raising prices is the fastest revenue lever you're ignoring — no new customers required.

details

What it is: Codie's six-lever framework for fast business growth, learned from buying and operating dozens of businesses — not from running one personal brand. The headline secret: the fastest growth lever has nothing to do with marketing.

The instructional secret — Lever 1: The Premium Flip

  • Stop competing on price, start competing on value
  • Most operators undercharge for years because they don't value themselves, not because the market won't pay
  • Case study: bought an online education business at $29/month, jacked it to $299/month (10x). Lost 30% of customers, annual revenue went way up
  • Math that should sting: a 20% price bump on a $1M business = $200K new revenue with zero new customers acquired
  • Bain study cited: 55% of surveyed companies could raise list prices and make more money

How to know you can raise prices:

  • You're selling out / can't keep inventory on shelves — market is signaling it'll pay more
  • You haven't raised prices in 12+ months — bare minimum 2–5% annual bump just to keep up with costs
  • Competitors charge more than you — price signals value, low prices signal cheap

Counter the objections:

  • "I'll lose customers" — price-sensitive customers are the worst customers; they churn for any cheaper option. Raising prices filters them out and surfaces your real fans
  • "Competitors will undercut me" — price shouldn't be your selling point. Aim for a mini-monopoly: unique value that lets you set your own prices
  • Louis Vuitton test: a $30 LV bag wouldn't be an LV bag. High prices filter penny-pinchers and signal value

Three revenue doublers to bolt on today:

  • Subscription — if you only sell one-time, add a recurring tier
  • Pricing tiers (low/medium/high) — most people pick medium, so price the medium where you actually want it. Add a high tier that feels uncomfortable to you; some people will buy it
  • Upsells — gum-at-the-checkout add-ons to lift LTV (lifetime value) and AOV (average order value)

Tools & links:

  • .Online domain $0.99 deal — sponsor link, cheap domain for testing a side hustle
  • Free 13-Week Cashflow Tracker — Codie's spreadsheet for tracking business cashflow

Why it matters for you: For a side hustle, your time is the constraint — you can't outwork a pricing problem. Test a price hike before you build another funnel; it's the highest-leverage move you can make in an afternoon.
